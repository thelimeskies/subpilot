# SubPilot Brand Identity

## Final Product Name

**SubPilot**

SubPilot is an independent subscription operations product for merchants and product teams. It uses Nomba APIs for payment collection, tokenized-card renewals, transfers, and payment webhooks, but it is not owned by Nomba.

## One-Liner

Subscription operations, guided from checkout to renewal.

## Short Pitch

SubPilot helps businesses launch and operate recurring billing without rebuilding subscription logic from scratch. Teams manage plans, billing cycles, proration, dunning, customer self-service, and developer webhooks in one place while Nomba APIs handle the payment infrastructure.

## Naming Rationale

- **Sub** signals subscriptions immediately.
- **Pilot** signals guidance, automation, and operational control.
- The name does not imply that SubPilot is a bank or payment processor.
- It leaves room to expand into usage billing, revenue recovery, and customer billing portals.

## Logo Concept

The logo mark is a **minimal S signal mark**:

- The S represents subscriptions and SubPilot.
- The rounded square makes it usable as an app icon, favicon, dashboard sidebar mark, and pitch-deck logo.
- The small teal signal suggests guidance and automation without adding visual noise.
- The active logo intentionally avoids badges, taglines, or Nomba integration text inside the lockup.
- The teal accent communicates control, clarity, and payment confidence without leaning on Nomba's visual identity.

## Typography

Recommended:

- App and docs: Inter
- Fallback: system UI, Segoe UI, Arial, sans-serif

Usage:

- Product wordmark: 800 weight
- Dashboard headings: 650-700 weight
- Body and table text: 400-500 weight
- Status labels: 650 weight

## Voice

SubPilot should sound:

- Clear
- Operational
- Trustworthy
- Specific
- Calm under failure states

Avoid:

- Sounding like a payment processor.
- Claiming ownership of Nomba APIs.
- Vague fintech language like "seamless payment revolution."

## Product Language

Use:

- "Uses Nomba APIs for payments"
- "Charge renewals with tokenized cards"
- "Recover failed invoices"
- "Preview proration before applying changes"
- "Replay signed webhooks"

Avoid:

- "Nomba Subscriptions"
- "Owned by Nomba"
- "Nomba native billing"
- "We are Nomba"

## Tagline Options

Primary:

- Subscription operations, guided from checkout to renewal.

Alternatives:

- Recurring billing without recurring rebuilds.
- Launch subscriptions. Recover revenue. Keep control.
- The control tower for subscription billing.
- Plans, renewals, recovery, and webhooks in one place.

## UI Branding Rules

- Use the logo mark in the sidebar and favicon.
- Use the horizontal wordmark on pitch and documentation cover pages.
- Use Signal Teal for primary actions and important operational focus states.
- Use Energy Accent sparingly for pitch callouts, not core finance states.
- Use status colors consistently and always pair color with a text label.
